## 2. New Additions: Co-Build & AV Fund

### Co-Build Section

**What it does:**
- Brand incubation arm of Acharya Ventures
- Helps founders build and scale brand identity alongside product
- Supports early-stage ventures with positioning, messaging, and go-to-market branding
- Bridges idea validation and market-ready brand presence

**Proposed content:**
> **Co-Build** — Brand Incubation for Founders  
> We help early-stage founders turn ideas into recognizable brands. From positioning and messaging to visual identity and go-to-market strategy, Co-Build supports ventures that are ready to stand out. Built for founders who want clarity and consistency before scale.

**Placement:** Dedicated section on Avlanding page 1, in the ecosystem flow (after Tools, before Capital).

---

### AV Fund Section

**What it does:**
- Long-term capital layer of the Acharya Ventures ecosystem
- Helps founders access funding through structured support
- Prepares ventures for institutional capital (pitch, data room, metrics)
- Connects validated founders with the right capital partners

**Proposed content:**
> **AV Fund** — Capital for Scale  
> Acharya Ventures supports founders in securing the funding they need to scale. Through preparation programs, investor readiness frameworks, and strategic introductions, we help ventures become fundable and connect with the right capital partners.

**Status:** **Coming Soon** — This feature will be activated as part of the ecosystem roadmap. Sign up to be notified when AV Fund launches.

**Placement:** Dedicated section on Avlanding page 1, with a clear "Coming Soon" badge and optional email signup for launch updates.
